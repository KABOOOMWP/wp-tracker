Privacy Policy

WP Padel Tracker  ·  Last updated: April 30, 2026

WP Padel Tracker is a padel match-scoring app for Apple Watch. This policy explains what information the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

1. Data We Collect

WP Padel Tracker collects no personal data and makes no network requests. The app operates entirely on your device. The only external data interaction is writing a workout record to Apple Health when a match is completed.

During a match the app holds the following data in memory only:

  • Current score, games, and sets
  • Serve rotation and server position
  • Match configuration (rules, play mode, number of sets)
  • In-match statistics (points played, break points, deuces)
  • Match start and end timestamps

When you complete a match, the app saves a single workout record to Apple Health (see section 3 below). No other data is written to disk or any external service.

2. Permissions Requested

The app requests only the HealthKit capability:

PermissionWhy it is needed
Health – Read Required by HealthKit to display your workout in the app's completion screen.
Health – Write Saves your completed padel match as a Tennis workout in Apple Health.

The app does not request internet access, location, camera, microphone, contacts, or any other sensitive permission beyond HealthKit.

3. HealthKit Integration

WP Padel Tracker uses Apple HealthKit exclusively to log a workout session when a match ends. The data written to Health is:

  • Activity type: Tennis (closest available category to padel)
  • Location type: Outdoor
  • Workout start time and end time

The app does not read heart rate, calories, distance, speed, or any other biometric measurement from HealthKit.

Health data written by this app is governed by Apple's Health app and your device's privacy settings. You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → WP Padel Tracker, or delete individual workout records directly in the Apple Health app.

Health data is never transmitted to the developer or any third party. It remains on your device (and in iCloud Health sync if you have enabled it) under Apple's control, not ours.

4. No Internet Access

The app makes zero network requests. No data ever leaves your device through this app.

5. No Third-Party Analytics or Tracking

WP Padel Tracker contains no analytics SDKs, crash-reporting services, advertising networks, or any other third-party tracking code. It uses only Apple system frameworks (HealthKit, WatchKit, SwiftUI, Foundation).

6. Data Retention

The developer does not retain any user data because no data is ever transmitted to or stored by the developer. Match state is held in device RAM and discarded when the app is closed. Workout records in Apple Health are under your control and can be deleted at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. It is safe for use by all age groups.
