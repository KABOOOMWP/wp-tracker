Privacy Policy

WP Padel Tracker  ·  Last updated: April 30, 2026

WP Padel Tracker is a padel match-scoring app for Wear OS watches. This policy explains what information the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

1. Data We Collect

WP Padel Tracker does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device with no internet connection.

During a match the app holds the following data in memory only:

  • Current score, games, and sets
  • Serve rotation and server position
  • Match configuration (rules, play mode, number of sets)
  • In-match statistics (points played, break points, deuces)
  • Match start and end timestamps

All of this data exists solely in RAM for the duration of the app session. It is never written to disk, a database, or any external service. When you close the app the data is gone.

2. Permissions Requested

The app requests only one permission:

  • Vibration (VIBRATE) – used to provide haptic feedback (e.g., a short buzz when a point is scored or a game is won). No data is read or collected through this permission.

The app does not request internet access, location, camera, microphone, storage, contacts, health data, or any other sensitive permission.

3. No Internet Access

The app has no INTERNET permission and makes zero network requests. No data ever leaves your device.

4. No Third-Party Analytics or Tracking

WP Padel Tracker contains no analytics SDKs, crash-reporting services, advertising networks, or any other third-party tracking code. The only third-party dependency is the Google Play Services Wearable library, which is required for Wear OS integration and does not collect data on behalf of this app.

5. Children's Privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. It is safe for use by all age groups.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is ever updated the new version will be published at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.

7. Contact

This privacy policy applies to the Wear OS version of WP Padel Tracker published on Google Play. For the watchOS (Apple Watch) version, see the Apple Watch privacy policy.
